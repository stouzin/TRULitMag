The TRU Literature Magazine is a student created and managed project focus on showcasing the creative works of Thompson Rivers University students in Kamloops, BC. The project was created in Winter of 2016 by Shane Touzin, Benjamin Lawrence, and Jonathan Bowden. Special thanks to all the student who have submitted work.
